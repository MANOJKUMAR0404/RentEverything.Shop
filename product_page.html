Product Description

The Canon EOS 5D Mark IV is a professional-grade DSLR camera that delivers exceptional image quality and performance. This versatile camera is ideal for a wide range of photography genres, from portraits and landscapes to sports and wildlife.

Featuring a 30.4-megapixel full-frame CMOS sensor and DIGIC 6+ image processor, it produces stunning images with remarkable detail and clarity. The camera offers an impressive ISO range of 100-32000 (expandable to 50-102400), allowing for exceptional low-light performance.

This rental includes the popular 24-70mm f/2.8L II USM lens, providing versatile focal length coverage suitable for most shooting scenarios. Whether you're a professional photographer needing a backup camera for an important shoot or an enthusiast wanting to try out high-end equipment, this rental is perfect for your needs.
